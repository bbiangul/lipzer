Bringing Stock trading Experience | to the DeFi Ecosystem

Lipzer is a Decentralized Trading Protocol operating on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), that enables traders, liquidity providers and developers to participate in an open financial market with no barriers to entry. We are going to make possible for the community to create buy or sell options (call or put) for cryptoassets, with specif deadline, quantity and prize.
