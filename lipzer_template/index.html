Bringing Stock trading Experience | to the DeFi Ecosystem

Lipzer is a Decentralized Trading Protocol operating on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), that enables traders, liquidity providers and developers to participate in an open financial market with no barriers to entry. We are going to make possible for the community to create buy or sell options (call or put) for cryptoassets, with specif deadline, quantity and prize.

OUR MAIN FEATURES

Invest

Your NFT option has earning interest!

Lipzer holders can create NFT options (call or put) to receive interests (rewards and transaction fees). In addition, you will earn prize when your NFT option has been sold on the marketplace.

Protection

Lock your risks!

Lipzer Exchange protocol enables traders to lock their risks by getting a PUT (NFT). In deadline date, you can choose to execute the option and sell your assets for a previous determined price.

Take your trading to the next level!
